# Landing Page Improvements - Minimalist Approach

**Philosophy:** Landing page SELLS, not educates. Keep it simple. Technical details → sales call.

**Created:** 2025-11-24

---

## PRINCIPLE: Less is More

**Good landing page:**
- ✅ Problem (emotional, simple)
- ✅ Solution (understand in 5 seconds)
- ✅ Results (specific numbers)
- ✅ Proof (social, cases)
- ✅ CTA (clear action)

**Bad landing page:**
- ❌ Timeline "month 1-2-3" (too detailed!)
- ❌ ROAS technical explanations (overwhelming!)
- ❌ "What we do weekly" with 20-100 operations (who cares?)

**Rule:** If you need to explain >5 seconds → NOT for landing page, it's for sales call!
