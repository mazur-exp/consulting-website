## PRIMARY PERSONA: "Alex the Foreign Restaurant Owner"

### Demographics:

**Name:** Alex (composite persona from real clients)
**Age:** 32-45 years old
**Nationality:** Russian (70%), Ukrainian (15%), European (10%), Other (5%)
**Language:** Russian or English primary, Indonesian basic or none
**Location:** Bali, Indonesia (Canggu 40%, Ubud 25%, Seminyak 15%, Uluwatu 10%, Sanur 10%)
**Education:** Higher education (often unrelated to restaurant business)
**Previous career:** Various (tech, marketing, construction, trading) - NOT professional restaurateur!

---

### Business characteristics:

**Business type:**
- Cafe, restaurant, or cloud kitchen
- Often started as "lifestyle business" (moved to Bali, opened cafe)
- Single location: 70% of clients
- 2-3 locations: 25% of clients
- 4+ locations: 5% of clients (these are "big clients")

**Team size:**
- Total: 5-15 people
- Kitchen: 3-8 people
- Service: 2-5 people
- Manager: 0-1 (often owner is manager!)

**Revenue:**
- Monthly total: $10,000-50,000
- Delivery %: 40-60% of total revenue
- Delivery revenue: $4,000-30,000/month
- After platform commissions: $3,000-22,500/month (this is what they get on account)

**Profitability:**
- Gross margin: 25-35% (after all costs)
- Often struggling with profitability (don't count delivery economics correctly!)

**Platforms used:**
- GoJek: 90% use
- Grab: 95% use
- Both: 85% use both

**Time in business:**
- New (1-6 months): 20% (struggling, need help urgently!)
- Established (6-24 months): 50% (growing, want to optimize)
- Mature (24+ months): 30% (stable, looking for next level)

### Psychographics:

**Goals:**
- Primary: Make good living on Bali ($5,000-15,000/month net)
- Secondary: Build sellable business (exit in 3-5 years for $200k-1M)
- Lifestyle: Enjoy Bali (beach, surfing, community), don't want to work 80h/week
- Recognition: Be known as "successful restaurateur" in local community

**Values:**
- Freedom (chose Bali for lifestyle!)
- Quality (want to serve good food, not just make money)
- Community (tight-knit expat community, reputation matters)
- Honesty (appreciate straight talk, hate manipulation)

**Fears:**
- Business failure (many cafes close after 6-12 months on Bali)
- Running out of money (burn rate high, revenue unpredictable)
- Rating drop → orders decrease → revenue crash → close business
- Being scammed (Bali has many "experts" who don't deliver)
- Losing independence (don't want to be managed, want to be owner!)

**Frustrations:**
- Platform complexity (Indonesian language!)
- Constantly putting out fires (orders, reviews, ads, staff problems)
- No time for strategic work (always operational!)
- Feel like "working IN business, not ON business"
- Discounts war (competitors always discount, kills margins)

**Aspirations:**
- Business runs without them (delegation!)
- Predictable revenue (know what to expect)
- High ratings and lots of orders (social proof in community)
- Time freedom (6-8h/day work, not 12-14h)
- Eventually: sell business or open more locations

### Pain points (specific!):

**Pain #1: Rating drop crisis**
- **Trigger:** Rating drops from 4.7 to 4.2 in 2 weeks
- **Impact:** Orders decrease 40%, revenue down $3,000-8,000/month
- **Emotion:** Panic! "What's happening? Why?!"
- **Current solution:** Try random things (discounts, new photos, beg for reviews)
- **Doesn't work:** Rating continues falling, don't know root cause

**Pain #2: Platform incomprehensibility**
- **Trigger:** Interface in Indonesian, support speaks poor English
- **Impact:** Waste 5-10 hours/week trying to figure out, make wrong decisions
- **Emotion:** Frustration! "I don't understand what I'm doing!"
- **Current solution:** Google translate, ask Indonesian friends, guess
- **Doesn't work:** Miss important features, don't optimize properly

**Pain #3: Ads lose money**
- **Trigger:** Tried running ads, ROAS <1 (spending $500, getting $300 back)
- **Impact:** Waste $1,000-3,000 on ineffective ads
- **Emotion:** Confusion! "Ads don't work? Or I'm doing wrong?"
- **Current solution:** Stop ads (miss growth!) or keep losing money
- **Doesn't work:** Either no growth or bleeding money

**Pain #4: Discount wars kill margin**
- **Trigger:** Competitors do 50% discount, owner feels pressure to match
- **Impact:** More orders BUT negative profit (don't realize until too late!)
- **Emotion:** Trapped! "Can't compete without discounts, but discounts kill me!"
- **Current solution:** Do discounts anyway, hope volume compensates
- **Doesn't work:** Math doesn't work out (30% food cost + 25% commission + 50% discount = loss!)

**Pain #5: No time for business development**
- **Trigger:** Spend 5-10h/week on delivery ops (prices, menu, ads, reviews)
- **Impact:** No time for strategic work (new products, partnerships, scaling)
- **Emotion:** Overwhelmed! "I'm a manager, not owner!"
- **Current solution:** Work 12-14h/day, burn out
- **Doesn't work:** Unsustainable, quality of life drops, health suffers

### Buying behavior:

**How they make decisions:**
- Consult with other owners (tight community, everyone knows everyone)
- Ask for recommendations in "Horeca Bali" Telegram group (800 members!)
- Google search "gojek grab консультант бали" (sometimes)
- Check social proof (how many clients? who uses service?)
- Skeptical (Bali has many scammers, they've been burned before)

**Decision timeline:**
- Research: 1-2 days (ask around, check reviews)
- Evaluation: 1 demo call (30-40 min)
- Decision: Same day or next day
- Closing: 1 closing call (30-40 min)
- **Total: 3 days from first touch to signing!** (very fast because leads are HOT)

**Decision makers:**
- Solo owner: Makes decision alone (60%)
- Co-owners: Discuss with partner, decide together (30%)
- Investor-backed: Need to consult investor (10%, longer cycle 1-2 weeks)

**What convinces them:**
1. **Social proof** - "85+ clients" → must work!
2. **Real numbers** - "+60%, +40%" → not vague promises
3. **No risk** - "10% of results, no upfront" → safe to try
4. **Time savings** - "0 hours" → I can focus on business
5. **Honesty** - "Month 1 ROAS <1 is normal" → they trust us

**What scares them away:**
1. **Hidden pricing** - "call us for price" → distrust
2. **Vague promises** - "we'll grow your business" → sounds like scam
3. **No social proof** - "we're experts" → says who?
4. **Pressure tactics** - "sign now or price goes up" → run away!
5. **Complexity** - too many options, packages, tiers → overwhelm, don't buy

### Lifetime value calculation:

**Average client:**
- Monthly fee: $600
- Lifetime: 10 months
- **LTV: $6,000** (before costs)
- Our costs: $80/month × 10 = $800
- **Net LTV: $5,200**

**With retention curve:**
- Month 0-6: 90% stay = 0.9 × $3,600 = $3,240
- Month 6-12: 70% stay = 0.7 × $3,600 = $2,520
- Month 12-18: 50% stay = 0.5 × $3,600 = $1,800
- **Weighted LTV: $7,560** (some stay longer!)

**Actually reported LTV:** $3,500 (52M IDR)
- This suggests many leave between 6-10 months
- Opportunity: improve retention Month 6-12 (70% → 80% = +$1,000 LTV!)
